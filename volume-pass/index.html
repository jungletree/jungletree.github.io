Volume Pass

Make rush geometry volume shape or color fit the gate ‘s one and across them!

Volume Pass is an agile category of parkour casual 3D games. In the game, the player needs to control the volume body through various forms of door frames. Depending on the game mode, the volume body needs to change colors or change shapes before passing the door frame successfully, so as to be consistent with the color or frame hole of the door frame. The game level is endless mode, keep alive longer, through the more door frames, and the higher the score you will get. So guys, come and give a try!

How to play:

  • Tap the screen to switch the shape or color

立方穿越

立方穿越是一款敏捷类的跑酷休闲3D游戏。游戏中玩家需要控制立方体积通过各种不同形式的门框，根据游戏模式的不同，立方体积在成功通过门框之前需要改变颜色或者改变形状，从而与门框的颜色或者框洞形状保持一致。游戏关卡为无尽模式，坚持的时间越长，通过的门框越多，分数也就越高。那么小伙们，快来挑战你的高分吧。

游戏玩法：

  • 点击屏幕切换立方体积形状或者颜色
