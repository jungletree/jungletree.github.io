Puzzle Snowman Screenshots:

Puzzle Snowman

Let’s play this amazing game for kids and adults. Rescue the snowman it’s amazing, adventure and brain puzzle game. Set the little snowman free from the ice blocks, and make it reach to the target successfully.

When winters comes, the day of snowman plays for fun also coming. But it seems our little snowman is trapped in some ice block matrices. Can you figure out and find the right way for it? What you need to do is moving the ice block up or down and find a clear way to his objective! As you move advance, the level will become harder and harder. Some ice blocks has it’ own relationship with it’s neighbors, they will move up and down with same direction or opposite direction. So you need to move it reasonably. Any way, you could play it anytime and anywhere. just relax and enjoy this puzzle game.

Game Features:

  • Multiple amazing levels included.
  • Click drag up an ice block to move it up, click and drag down it to move it down.
  • Christmas spirit music
  • Great graphics
  • Suitable for relax
  • addictive game-play

雪人之谜 - 雪人华容道

雪人之谜是一款烧脑的益智冒险游戏。游戏主人公是一个非常可爱的小雪人。冬天来临时，贪玩的小雪人被困在了一些冰块矩阵之中。 你需要巧妙合理地移动这些冰块，开辟一条到达小雪人目的地的道路–小雪人的华容道，从而解救被困的小雪人。

游戏看似简单，然后事实并非如此。随着游戏关卡的递进，关卡难度逐步上升，这些冰柱之间的关系变得有点棘手了。例如，移动一个冰块时，它周边的冰块也可能随之一起动，或者朝着相反的方向运动。不管怎么样，在您多尝试几次后总会找到谜题的答案。相信你能为它找出一条正确的到达目的地的路径！

游戏特色:

  • 多个精心设计的游戏关卡
  • 点击并向上拖动冰块将其向上移动，单击并向下拖动以将其向下移动
  • 有冬天圣诞节氛围的音乐效果
  • 不错的画面表现
  • 操作简单，引人入胜的游戏玩法
  • 适合放松或者用来打发时间
