Badminton World Champion 2018

Compete with badminton players from all over the world and smash to competitor!

Badminton is one of the most popular sports game in the world! In Crazy Badminton, compete with badminton players from all over the world! Do a crazy smash to your competitor!

Take on your friends in 1 vs 1 mode, or win the Badminton League trophy in Tournament Mode!
Customizing your character with tons of items. And level up your ability to do stronger smash and jump!
Now grab your racket to smash, hit the shuttlecock, do a crazy smash to your opponent like badminton star!
Also there are many sympathetic audience cheering for you.
Also you can learn some tips from the game,such as Shuttle Run. The purpose of the shuttle run is to move to the different areas of the court while playing. You do not need to use a racket for this exercise.
You will always end with your racket foot. After picking up each shuttle, return to the center. This exercise teaches you to have a ‘feeling’ of where you are on the court based on the number of steps you are taking.
It also develops your fitness on court. Remember to always cross the center.

  • Cool stunts and realistic hitting shuttlecock experience
  • Numerous gorgeous badminton outfits

羽毛球世界冠军赛2018

羽毛球世界冠军赛:找准落脚点,轻轻点击屏幕即可让主角将球拍过球网，规则与现实生活中的羽毛球赛事一样

这是一款画面精美的体育竞技类羽毛球游戏。在游戏中，玩家只需找准落脚点，轻轻点击屏幕即可让主角将球拍过球网，规则与现实生活中的羽毛球赛事一样。

真实的比赛体验搭配各种炫酷的击球方式，让羽毛球赛在手机上也能畅快地进行！

游戏拥有多种不同的游戏模式，你可以在普通模式中通过单打或者双打来赚取金币，也可以在练习模式中提升自己的技巧，还有锦标赛模式、多人模式让你可以和其他玩家进行比拼。

游戏支持定制自己的角色，你还可以通过在商店购买球拍，鞋子，服装等道具来让自己的角色变得更强，喜欢羽毛球的玩家不妨来下载试试吧！
