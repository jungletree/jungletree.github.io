Uphill Cargo Driver 3D

Uphill Cargo Driver 3D is the new edition truck cargo game in 2019 and it’s a game that tests the player’s driving skills. The difference between this and other truck driving games is that you can experience the winding and dangerous mountain road in the truck race game. As a cargo loaded truck driver, whoever you are, truck girls or truck boys, you should drive carefully in the rough road. Transport Customer’s luggage safely. Do not overload a wagon. You released the clutch and the truck began to move. Various types of trucks for you to choose from. Minivan, heavy truck, and more. It depend on you.
This is the most realistic truck game in market. Deliver heavy cargo from one place to another for sterling. Drive to explore off-road environment and test your driving skills. If you want to became a truck driver and this is impossible for some reason for you, you have to try this game.
It is impossible for you to drive a heavy truck at the beginning. You have to practice your driving skills. Actually minivan is a good choice. Your mission is to carry luggage. Do not overload. A caterpillar must pass through the cocoon stage to become a butterfly. You also need pass through difficulties and to be the king of the road.
These roads will test your driving capability and will also enable you to increase your skill and experience on the road. Drive carefully through the dark night, in foggy weather and in thundering rain. Clutch the steering wheel and move carefully step by step in rough road. You have to drive these super huge trucks on dangerous roads in the mountains where even one mistake could lead to fatal consequences but don‘t worry this is just a driving game.
You get to drive transporting trucks on hills loaded with goods as you race to get them to the destination. Blared your horn to drive out animals. The hill driving can be exciting and adventurous as you have to maneuver and drive the heavy truck along hills and dangerous paths. Take care that you truck slowly on the mountain road, do not let the cargo hanged fall off, and avoid the surrounding masts. Watch out for bumps and sharp turns as they can make the load fall off the trailer.
Show your driving skills by safely transporting the objects to the destination without dropping any on the way. Carry heavy cargo across the site safely and park your trucks to complete the game. Play the best driving games of 2018. Lots of cargo items like drums, wooden pallets & gas cylinders etc.
As a minivan driver, you have to keep the luggage safely. Careless use of the clutch may damage the gears. When you’ve done your job well, you can be allowed to drive heave truck. Work hard and try your best to be the king of road. Remember never overload, it will increase the odds of failure. To challenge a master. Don’t be afraid. He was not a paragon. He would never be perfect. You drive heavy truck. deliver supplies to the army. The game tracks is saved in real time, so don’t worry about forgetting to save them. Enjoy the cargo truck game, it will give you more realistic experience than other truck driving games.

  • Different Truck Models

  • Excellent graphics
  • Reverse and forward transmission

当你跑到目的地的时候，你可以在山上开着运输卡车，满载货物。在山上开车可以是令人兴奋和冒险的，因为你必须驾驶重型卡车沿山丘和危险的道路。
