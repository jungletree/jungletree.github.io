Lost Tomb Treasures

Lost Tomb Treasures is an adventure game with logical elements!
Be ready to long, unforgiven trip in uncharted places. In the jungles, filled mystical secrets, behind ancient ruins, tombs and dungeons, with many danger traps on a way. Smart labyrinths come to magic Mayan temple with mysterious treasures in a final.

Impeccably finish levels, become nimble tomb rider, and receive a gold coin. Collect items, artifacts and jewelry, to help in the hunt for treasure in the passing game. In addition to exciting gameplay, the game will involve you in the gameplay its intriguing storyline with an unpredictable ending.
Goal of the game to find the best way through the maze with obstacles, not forgetting to collect all the treasures hidden in the level. Each level is filled with unique traps and enemies.

Features:

  • Colorful 3D graphics, a huge map with changing locations
  • Smart gameplay, diverse sets of traps on logic and reaction
  • The storyline with funny comics will relax your brain after difficult puzzles
  • Three long episodes. Adventure never ends - constantly come out with new episodes, even more interesting puzzles, new traps, new stories!
    …and much more!

失落遗迹的宝藏

“失落遗迹的宝藏”是一个带有智力逻辑元素的冒险游戏！
准备好去未知的地方开启一段漫长而又难以忘怀的旅行吧。在充满了神秘的秘密的丛林里，在找到古代废墟后面、墓地和地牢的途中布满了许多危险的陷阱。接着会遇到智慧神迷宫，最后终于到达包含有神秘宝藏神奇的玛雅神殿。

近乎完美地完成关卡，成为灵活的古墓骑士，并获得金币。收集物品，文物和珠宝，以帮助在通关游戏中寻找宝藏。 除了令人兴奋的游戏玩法之外，游戏还将让您参与到迷人故事情节中，并带来难以预料的结局。
游戏的目标是通过障碍物找到通过迷宫的最佳途径，不要忘记收集隐藏在关卡中的所有宝藏。每个关卡都充满了独特的陷阱和敌人。

游戏特征：

  • 丰富多彩的3D图形，包含有延伸变化的地点的巨大地图
  • 有趣的游戏方式，各种的逻辑谜题和陷阱
  • 有趣的漫画故事情节将困难解惑后放松你的大脑
  • 三个故事长片。冒险永远不会结束 - 不断出现新的剧集，甚至更有趣的谜题，新的陷阱，新的故事！
    …以及其他更多内容！
