Twisty Truck Driver 3D

Do you like truck driving games?
Twisty Truck Driver 3D is one of the truck driving games that you will deliver cargo on mountain roads. Twisty roads, risky ramps and downhills will make you feel like driving a real truck. It’s time to drive the truck to complete all routes. Heavy cargoes will cause truck to fall into the cliff.These features will make this driving simulator the best on truck games. Download now for fun.

Features

  • Steering Wheel, Buttons or Tilting controls
  • Realistic 3D graphics and physics
  • Day and night maps
  • Mountain roads
  • Hill climb
