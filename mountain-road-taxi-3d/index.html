Mountain Road Taxi 3D

crazy taxi driver on zigzag risky snow mountain road, taxi driving simulator

Mountain Road Taxi 3D is the latest and the greatest taxi game of all the taxi driving games. In the driver game, you should pick up nice passengers from the hill station resorts, going through a rugged risky mountain road,enjoying fantastic scenery. Signal a turn with your blinker.Train your skills and to be a pro taxi driver.
In these Asian countries the city taxi is the cheapest way of passenger transportation from one taxi stop to another. Other taxi games not contain these types of Offroad taxi games features. You will become taxi master in this jeep game by showing your taxi parking skills with your Indian taxi or any other transport car or jeep drive. Perform efficient cab driving with your taxi 2018 and become hero of all taxi games. Be an Indian taxi driver because once the passenger sit in your taxi car then you can collect coins from the risky road and steep paths.
Hill Station is packed with thrilling and exciting pick and drop missions. Pick up passengers from distant locations and take them to their destination on time. The sights of snowy rocky hills will add enjoyment to your journey.
In this taxi drive, you can see the mesmerizing and green hill environments. Perform your duty in this taxi simulator game skillfully to enjoy your taxi ride. This real taxi 3d needs skills of professional taxi driver with limited fuel. So,be quantitative during taxi driving with your off-road drive. Drive a powerful taxi cab in any kind of environment like Off-road Mountains and city traffic. So, enjoy cab drive with smooth and realistic controls like a real taxi on the environment. Good Luck.
In the beginning, you own a basic taxi and by picking up passengers, you can buy a more sophisticated taxi.?Be a polite driver, say hi to a passenger when he gets on the bus and drunk before you drive is not allowed. You can take passengers to the scenic spots where they wants to visit and while on the risky road you can turn on the Intel player and listen to music. As a qualified taxi driver, remember to keep your words.
Drive with real 4x4 taxi cars, making sure your wheels gain the best traction and power possible. However the peculiar nature of this taxi car beast means that it’s not always possible to drive careless. So we hope you will have fun playing this taxi driving game . It will never be a drag getting a couple of tonnes of car up these risky road and complete the duties you have as a cab driver.
Enjoy the hill car driving, the game can give you fun which other taxi games can’t give you.

The features of Mountain Road Taxi 3D include:

  • Summer and Winter Season Environments
  • Amazing Hill Station Environments and 3D Graphics
  • Thrilling Precision Taxi Driving Experience
  • A lot of taxi drive in the garage.
  • Addictive game play.
  • Fun and challenging levels

山道出租车3D

冬季雪山蛇形弯曲公路上的疯狂客运出租车司机，山路出租车驾驶模拟

这是你一直在寻找最好的出租车模拟游戏。您驾驶您的出租车在驾驶舱浏览并沉浸在优美的环境中。感受新的无止境的越野冒险。倾斜您的设备来控制您的出租车，赚取硬币、购买新的出租车和升级出租车。你可能玩过不同的爬坡游戏，但这次你肯定会享受更多乐趣。这是最好的最令人上瘾的游戏，在隆隆的汽车声中，感受最真实的出租车驾驶体验。
通过改进的物理引擎，你会感觉到最接近于顶层的真实模拟。有新模式和无尽模式，独特和不同的8大气候等待着你去发现。真实天气条件下的冒险等着你。驾驶各种出租车在泥泞的道路上。发动所有车轮驱动，当很难行驶时，用绞盘把你自己从麻烦中拉出来。
在山上开车可以是令人兴奋和冒险的，因为你必须驾驶重型出租车沿着山和危险的令人感到眩晕的小径行驶。注意在山路上慢慢行驶，避免涡轮螺旋桨飞机，并避免周围的桅杆和摩托车，注意颠簸和急转弯。
小心别让轮胎卡在泥里了。开车穿过山谷，穿过泥泞的小路。成为一名有很强驾驶技能的司机。记住要安全驾驶。不要超载。
