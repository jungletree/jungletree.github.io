Extreme Falling 3D

Welcome to this super extreme falling racing game, fasten your seat belts and get yourself ready to surf through this infinite and a never-ending tunnel. hit any obstacles and you lost. it uses the orientation sensor or screen side touch as controls, making it very direct and intuitive.

“Extreme Falling 3D” is specially designed for the people who love real racing dash games. the objective is to dash through a twisting color tunnel full of obstacles at a high-speed. there is no margin of error in this fast-paced death pipe environment. your race is instantly over as soon as you make a mistake and hit yourself with an obstacle coming your way. making it out alive from this playground tunnel is almost impossible. the challenge is how far can you go and how much time you can survive in this death pipe? don’t forget to collect gems on your way through to get points.

Extreme Falling 3D seem easy but hard to play. your goal is to just tap on right moment to move direction left,right. this game require your concentration to survive if you lose it at any point, the game will take back you from where you start.

Extreme Falling 3D is a reflex game where you avoid obstacles to get as far as you can. Race through endless twisting tunnels in the ultimate test of your racing skills. The game offers many challenging game modes. Rush down the colorful tunnel at high speed, collect gold and gems, use your special abilities wisely. Simply download for fun! Enjoy it!

  • fast-paced, endless fun and real racing game-play
  • colorful and eye catchy graphics used
  • movie quality sounds and music
  • simple and easy tilt controls
  • game is free to install and play
