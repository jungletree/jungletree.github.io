Real Excavator Simulator 3D

Drive, Dig, Transport and live like a city builder in this game.

Get ready to live like a city builder in Real Excavator Simulator 3D game. This new city construction site game offers you variety of road builder and sand excavator trucks each with their own mission and difficulty.
From the experience of driving a bulldozer simulator to being a PRO crane operator and maneuvering a loader dump truck. Real Excavator Simulator 3D offers you all sort of road construction trucks and cranes
for your sand excavator task. Take your chance with this city builder game and enjoy the fun of multiple construction city simulator all in one game.
As the construction worker of your own city construction site, showcase your professtional skills, when you take control of the construction crew like bulldozer, backhoe, excavator or even loader truck.
No wonder you have some experience about river sand excavation, construction and builder with many of our city construction crane operator games.
But this Real Excavator Simulator 3D is made specially to give you the best of sand excavator, road construction and city builder experience.
Once you get behind the steering wheel of the sand excavator vehicles all your focus should be only on how to complete the construction simulator task effectively and efficiently.
Packed with full controls of heavy excavator dumper truck and other construction cranes, this game will give you a hand on experience of maneuvering hydraulic river sand excavator crane using steering and other controls.

Features of Real Excavator Simulator 3D:

  • Real Construction Site Environment 3D
  • Superior Heavy Excavator Controls
  • Drive Excavator Crane Like a Pro
  • Challenging City Construction Levels
  • Drive, Dig, Transport and More

挖掘机模拟大师3D

挖掘机模拟大师3D是一款模拟挖掘机的手机游戏，挑战危险级别的挖掘机特技，开不了真的挖掘机我们就在手机上体验一下就好了。

在游戏中你可以挑战国际挖掘机大赛的真实挑战项目，比如上台阶、走钢丝等，以及比实际更难，更变态的项目。当然还少不了常规的挖掘机项目比如挖土、装载等,你可以体验到真实的挖掘机挖土效果；

快来开启神奇的挖掘机冒险之旅吧！

游戏特色：

1、高仿真的3D挖掘机，挖掘、移动、转向等等。
2、顶尖的环境效果，泥土、水池、AI汽车等等。
3、经典的现场施工模拟，以及趣味十足的特技挑战。
4、多角度视角自由旋转，放大、缩小随时变换。
5、车内视角提供更真实的第一人称驾驶体验。
