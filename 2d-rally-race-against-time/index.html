2D Rally Race Against Time

racing car climbing mountain simulation/simulate
You drive a buggy to a rugged mountain range, ready for an extreme racing challenge, driving a buggy on a hilltop, moving to a target location within a specified amount of time, constantly gaining new distances and challenging higher points; and paying attention , Here’s buggy is no brake, only the throttle. You can only choose to accelerate forward.

2D RALLY FEATURES

  • 2D Rally: Race Against Time is a 2D racing game with one touch controls
  • Procedurally generated hill tracks with check points
  • Compare your rally racing skills against others and share your highscore to friends
  • NO in-app purchases
  • In game leaderboard. Can you beat your friend’s highscore?
  • In Play achievements: distance, speed and more, compare your progress against friends
  • Quick reset in case you crash your rally car
  • Additional race and player stats for advanced drivers: collected medals, longest drive, average speed, high speed and crashes

2D RALLY GAME GUIDE

  • Tap and hold the screen to start racing. Keep it down to give car more speed
  • Try to get your car as far as possible, as fast as possible before you run out of time. Try to finish a race without many crashes
  • Release touch to slow down speed. Handy against steep hills
  • If you crash your car and get stuck, wait till the car resets automatically to continue the race.
  • Open the game menu and click the rewind button to restart a race
  • To drive further, a check point will increase your time
  • If you are out of time, you may still make it to a check point if the car is rolling downhill!
  • After a game over, a line segment will show your previous best drive and current drive

限时越野赛

越野车爬坡模拟游戏
你驾驶着越野车来到了崎岖的山脉，准备进行一次极限的赛车挑战，驾驶越野车在山顶飞驰，在规定的时间内跑到目标地点，不断的获取新的距离，挑战更高积分；并且注意，这里的越野车是没有刹车的，只有油门。你只能选择加速前行。

游戏特色

  • 限时越野赛是一款单点触控操作的2d赛车游戏
  • 通过玩家成绩表现程序动态生成山路
  • 和朋友比较赛车技巧，以及向你的朋友分享你的成绩
  • 没有应用程序内购买
  • 游戏带有全球排行榜。看看你和你的朋友谁更厉害？
  • 游戏内实时成就：距离、速度、进度和其他方面，可以与你的朋友比较一番
  • 发生碰撞翻车时，快速重置
  • 关于高级车手的其他赛车和队员统计数据：奖牌收集，最长驾驶距离，平均车速，最高速和碰撞次数

游戏玩法

  • 点击并按住屏幕开始比赛。 保持按下屏幕，让汽车更快
  • 在有限时间耗尽之前让汽车尽可能地快。 尝试完成一场没有碰撞翻车的比赛
  • 释放触摸以减慢速度。这方便应对陡峭的小山
  • 如果您的车辆发生碰撞并卡住了，请等待车辆自动重置以继续比赛。
  • 打开游戏菜单并点击“后退按钮”重新开始比赛
  • 每个检查点会增加你的驾驶时间，让你开得更远
  • 如果时间到了，汽车下坡，你可能仍然会进入检查点，继续进行驾驶。
  • 游戏结束后，线段将显示您以前的最佳驾驶距离和当前驾驶距离
