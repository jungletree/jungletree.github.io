Knight Grail

In the game a knight was ordered to find the Holy Grail. The Holy Grail is in the depths of a dark dungeon and is guarded by various traps and skeleton warriors. To gain the Holy Grail, the knight must use his wisdom and perseverance to overcome all kinds of dangers and obstacles and to reach the location of the grail. Will knight finally get the Holy Grail it? Come and give a try?

How to play:

  • Press the screen to move the knight forward (brave knight fearless, he always moves forward, his dictionary does not contain “turn back” words)

骑士与圣杯

游戏中一名骑士奉命去寻找圣杯。圣杯位于暗黑地牢的深处，并且被各种各样的机关和骷髅兵守卫着。想要获得圣杯，骑士必须运用智慧和毅力克服各种各样的危险和障碍，成功到到达地牢深处。骑士最终能否获得圣杯么？赶快来挑战一下吧？

游戏玩法：

  • 按下屏幕朝前移动骑士（勇敢的骑士无所畏惧，他总是勇往直前，他的字典里没有“后退”二字）
