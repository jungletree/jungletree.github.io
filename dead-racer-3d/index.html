Dead Racer 3D

Can you survive in the world destroyed after a nuclear war? With radiation, hunger and diseases around you. You have to cross the whole country and save your family. who knows if they are alive, or maybe radiation and deadly virus have already reached them? reveal a secret of the apocalypse and get your memories back during a journey across the huge territory of the ussr in the 80th.

In game, you need to drive your way through a zombie apocalypse. Fire your guns and kill all zombies in your path. Avoid other cars and obstacles and get as far as you can.
‘Dead Racer 3D’ allows you to choose your car and upgrade it. Personalize your car by changing its weapon or its armor. Also, you can buy new car models in the store.
Kill all zombies in your way, gather as many coins as you can, and upgrade your ride in this amazing game!

It is not easy to survive after apocalypse. You’ll face the real hungry games! monsters, zombie, thirst, uncountable diseases and injuries, blood-thirsty enemies – You have to fight it all. Craft all your resources: weapons, clothes, means of transport.

Scout through immersive environments, engage in rapid-fire combat and build the ultimate super-soldier as you customise their state of the art gear.
Team up with wolf, jackson and the rest of strike force as you target hostiles and take down the evil elite order.

Features:

  • Lots of zombie killing fun.
  • Great cars & vehicles and guns & weapons:
  • Free and customize your vehicle and weapon as you wish
  • Good HD colorful graphics.
  • Simple control & easy to get start.
  • Multiple amazing design levels

死亡车手3D

当病毒蔓延整个世界，无数的人变成丧尸，作为一名幸存者，在危机四伏的丧尸城中，你唯一能做的只有猎杀丧尸、拯救你自己以及其他的幸存者！
游戏背景设定在未来末日世界，玩家驾驶战车在充满丧尸的公路上高速飞驰。沿途，你需要开枪扫射并杀死所有的僵尸，反应迅速敏捷躲避路中报废的车辆以及其他障碍物，同时尽可能快速地驾驶。

想成为僵尸之城的侠胆车手么?快来免费体验驾车驰骋横扫僵尸的畅快吧!

‘死亡车手3D’可以让你选择购买车辆，并对它进行个性化的改装：改装它的装备和武器。当然你也可以在商店里购买新的车辆。
干掉沿途的所有僵尸，收集尽可能多的硬币，然后升级你的座驾！置身僵尸群中,拥有一款强劲、操控灵活的座驾,是安全的最根本保证。行云流水般地穿越城市废墟, 连续碾压、射杀僵尸以获得更高能力, 不断挑战自我极限,创造更高纪录!

游戏中，你可以获得各式汽车甚至是坦克，你也可以改装它们，为它们装上各种武器，但你更要保护好它们，躲避开途中的障碍物，车辆是你逃生的唯一工具。
途中还会有许多丧尸袭击你，不要吝啬你的子弹，杀光它们，快速冲出去！
你能逃出生天吗？

游戏特点：

  • 体验猎杀僵尸的畅快淋漓快感。
  • 许多的可供驾驶的汽车等车辆和各种各样的枪械武器。
  • 自由改装和组合的车辆和武器
  • 操作简单，容易上手
  • 不错的图形表现。
  • 易于上手的游戏玩法。
  • 多个精心设计的游戏关卡
