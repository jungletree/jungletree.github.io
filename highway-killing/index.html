Highway Killing

Highway Killing a most thrilling first-person shooter game. Player serve as an excellent sniper in the game. Taking the vantage place, seeking for terrorists, and eliminate them. All kinds of weapons in store are available to choose. Long-range weapons can cause fatal damage to the enemy. Your enemies appear in the highway. Some of them drive off-road car on road. Find them and pull the trigger immediately. I am sure you will be the best sniper.
You are a well-trained elite sniper, accepting secret missions, and eliminating terrorists. In this exciting game, experience the fun of shooting, zoom in, aim, kill. Pull the trigger and try for the head shot - at range.
As the levels goes up, the mission gets tougher and the rewards get higher. equip yourself with powerful weapons and shoot them down to be the best shooter. You can search for targets based on red arrows, completing missions within the given time. Use your huge reward to unlock your favorite from all kinds of options like Shooting guns, war weapons, assault rifles, main auxiliary weapons and sniper rifles. The battlefield is full of crisis, they are cruel. They also try to find and strike you. I am sure you can escape all the attacks, to complete all the challenges.
This shooting game in this virtual environment will bring you different experiences. As a clever killer, use you your limited ammunition to kill every enemy. The enemy does not know your presence until you pull the trigger. Swat the enemy helicopters out of the sky like flies. The critical thing for you is how to kill all the enemies. Practice your skills more, you will be the king of this game.
Game features:

  • Awesome 3D graphics.
  • Awesome and realistic simulations.
  • Heavy action and blood.
  • Great battle situations.
  • Multiple sniper guns available.

公路杀戮

公路杀戮是一款激动人心的第一人称射击游戏。玩家在游戏中充当出色的狙击手。占据有利位置，寻找恐怖分子并消灭他们。商店里有各种武器可供选择。远程武器会对敌人造成致命伤害。你的敌人出现在高速公路上。他们中的一些人在公路上开越野车。找到他们并立即扣动扳机。我相信你会是最好的狙击手。
你是一个训练有素的精英狙击手，接受秘密任务，消灭恐怖分子。在这个激动人心的游戏中，体验射击、放大、瞄准、暗杀的乐趣。扣动扳机，尝试一枪爆头。随着级别的提高，任务变得更加艰巨，回报也越来越高。给自己装备上强大的武器，并击落他们，成为最好的射手。您可以根据红色箭头搜索目标，在给定的时间内完成任务。使用你的巨大奖励，打开你最喜欢的枪支。各种选择，如射击枪，战争武器，突击步枪，主要辅助武器和狙击步枪。战场上充满了危机，他们是残酷的。他们也试图找到并击打你。我相信你能逃脱所有的攻击，完成所有的挑战。
这个射击游戏在这个虚拟环境中会给你带来不同的体验。作为一个聪明的杀手，用你有限的弹药杀死每一个敌人。直到你扣动扳机，敌人才知道你的存在。像拍苍蝇一样猛击敌人的直升机。对你来说最关键的是如何杀死所有的敌人。多练习你的技能，你将成为这个游戏的王者。
游戏特色:
-不错的3D图形表现。
-极好和现实的模拟。
-最棒的行动和流血。
-逼真的狙击情形再现。
-提供多款狙击枪械
