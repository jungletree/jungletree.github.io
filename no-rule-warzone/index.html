No Rule WarZone

This game is a classic hardcore FPS games, in which you can complete combat sniper stealth missions, you can explore 20 stealth shooter levels with 3 themes like
City, Port and Snow, you have to destroy all the enemies in a limited time to complete the mission, you can test your shooting ability and combat skills to become
a real shooter!

You are a warrior, you have excellent fighting skills. Now that terrorists have occupied the city, you have to find and destroy them before the terrorists destroy
the city. The army provides you with advanced super weapons, so you can more easily complete the mission. The mission is very difficult, but for those innocent
people, you have to come forward, destroy the enemy, defend the city.

Accurate aiming at the target, firing, more accurate elimination of the surrounding terrorists. Ready to fight with the terrorists desperately, and strive to
shoot. In order to make you better fight with terrorists, your troops prepared a lot of advanced weapons for you. You are a front-line commando, have excellent
combat skills, show all your skills, penetrate the enemy’s territory, find and destroy hidden goals.

As the levels progress, the mission gets tougher and the rewards get higher. So equip yourself with the best gun and hunt them down to be the top shooter.
Search for targets based on radar instructions; completing missions within a specified period of time. Use your huge reward to unlock your favorite from a
variety of options like Shooting guns, war weapons, assault rifles, main / auxiliary weapons and Sniper rifles. The battlefield is full of crisis, all kinds
of cutting-edge weapon after another, whether you can escape all the attacks, to complete all the challenges.

Game Features:

  • the real military environment
  • all kinds of modern weapons
  • Challenging missions
  • realistic shooting effect
  • Smart AI
  • amazing music and sound effects
  • Fast-paced shooting game

法外之地

这是一款经典的第一人称射击游戏，在这款游戏里面，你可以完成各类狙击潜入任务，完成20多关不同主题的射击任务，主题包含城市，码头，雪地。你必须在规定的时间内击败所有的
敌人，你也可以通过游戏锻炼你的射击能力和战斗技巧，最终成为一名真正的射击者，击败恐怖分子，保卫自己的家园。

现在你是一名勇敢的士兵，你准备好战斗在真实的战场恐怖分子？现在是时候出击，拿起你的武器，向前走，在战场上战斗。摧毁敌人的残暴，完成使命。

你的城市受到了威胁。恐怖分子潜入你的城市和制造混乱。你作为一个突击队的前锋，接受军队的使命。消灭谁混进城所有的恐怖分子，拯救无辜的人，恢复城市的和平。
这将是一个艰巨的任务，你必须使用你的高超的射击技巧和作战能力，在战场上生存和完成任务。

军队为您提供各种先进和强大的武器，让你可以更容易地消除恐怖分子，完成使命。有先进的雷达系统，使您更好地寻找所有的敌人。一个强大的狙击镜头可以搜索更远的目标。
当你完成任务后，你将进入下一个任务，任务逐渐变得困难。展示你高超的射击技能，完成所有的任务，成为一个真正的狙击手。

游戏特色：

  • 真实的战场环境
  • 射击武器，特殊武器，重武器，突击步枪，主副武器
  • 挑战射击任务
  • 高清图像
  • 惊人的音乐和声音效果
  • 一款刺激好玩的射击游戏，游戏采用了逼真的3D画风
  • 玩家需要化身为一名特种部队的士兵，使用各种枪械执行任务，消灭所有敌人
