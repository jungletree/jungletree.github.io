Trials Gold 3D

Trials Gold 3D is an excellent and entertaining trials game with awesome stunts, jump, roll, dash and slide over to avoid amazing obstacles while you master the tricky tracks. Race through these dangerous levels without losing your control to unlock exciting new levels in this addictively fun trials game. This game has some great graphics and tons of cool things to look at, and has 20 amazing new addicting levels and 3 bikes.
This Trials Gold 3D game, will take your breath away with its new innovations and new amazing stunts and its smooth game-play will make you play this game for hours.

Game Features:

  • Real world 3D environment
  • Realistic controls
  • 20 Levels with scaling difficulty
  • Ultimate Trials challenges
  • High resolution graphics for both phones and tablets

极限摩托特技3D

极限摩托特技3D是一款优秀且有趣的摩托驾驶游戏，它包含了怎么样驾驭棘手的赛道，驾驶摩托展现特技，跳跃，翻滚，冲刺以及滑行来避免惊人的障碍。在这个容易上瘾的赛车游戏中，你需要比赛通过这些危险的关卡，同时又不失去你对摩托车的控制，这样可以解锁令人兴奋的新关卡。这个游戏包含有不错的图形表现和一些看起来很酷的东西，它共有20个惊人的新上瘾的关卡和3辆摩托车。

该极限摩托特技3D游戏，将让你感受到极限特技的惊险与刺激。感兴趣的小伙伴来试试吧！

游戏特色：

  • 取材于真实世界的3D环境
  • 简单直观的控制
  • 带有不同难度的20个关卡
  • 摩托特技终极挑战
