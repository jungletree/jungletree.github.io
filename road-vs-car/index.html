Road Vs Car

crazy roadeo rush of car chase

Road Vs Car is a very special racing game about the road and car. There are two modes provided in this game: free road and drive car. In free road mode, players need to controls direction and ascent of the constantly growing roadway without crash into any buildings, make sure keep as long possible. In drive car mode, the player tries to keep his race car following the constantly growing roadway for as long as possible. Both of them are endless mode, so that suit for time-killing. And a global online leaderboards included, so that you could beat your high score with other players.

The game graphics is fresh and elegant, I guess you will like it!

So guys, come and give a try, and see how high score you will get?

How to play:

  • touch screen / tilt screen to steer roadway direction
  • tilt screen to steer car

道路与车

疯狂道路冲刺与赛车追逐者的搭配
“道路与车”是一款关于道路和赛车的,非常有趣的赛车游戏。
这个游戏提供了两种模式：驾驶赛道和驾驶汽车。在驾驶赛道模式时，玩家需要控制不断增长的道路的方向，而不能让它撞到任何建筑物，并确保尽可能保持较长时间。在驾驶汽车模式中，玩家尽可能较长时间地将赛车跟随在不断增长的道路上。这两种玩法都是无尽的,方便用来打发时间. 同时游戏内提供了一个全球排行榜，以便您可以与其他玩家一较高下.

游戏画面清新丽雅,我相信你会喜欢它的!

那么骚年们，来试试看看你会得到多高的分数吧？

游戏玩法:

  • 点击屏幕,或者倾斜屏幕来驱动赛道方向
  • 倾斜屏幕来驱动赛道赛车前进方向
