Physics Ball: Balance Challenge

In Physics Ball: Balance Challenge game, you are to operate a ball, simultaneously keeping its balance. It is not so easy: your way runs over the clouds across narrow roads and air tracks or something like unconnected sky bridges. React fast, don’t cross the edges of the field, lest you should slide down to the chasm! Intermediate results are saved by the checkpoints automatically - if you fall it will bring you to the last check point.

Every new stage will bring you new challenges: steep slopes and bends, dangerous areas, barriers - there is no time for boredom. Each level built with high level graphics quality, you could explore the world with camera zoom in/out view operation, and see more details.
Be attentive and patient, and you will succeed in winning the game at the end!

How to play:

  • easy to get start
  • great colorful HD graphics
  • addictive game play
  • various and amazing game levels
  • slide the left screen to roll ball
  • slide the right screen to tune the view camera angle

物理平衡球

在物理平衡球游戏中，您需要滚动一个平衡球，同时保持其平衡。这不是听起来那么容易：你需要操作它穿过云层上狭窄的空中栈道或者是没有相互连接在一起的天桥。快速反应，不要让天空之球越过轨道的边缘，否则你会滑落峡谷深处！游戏中间过程会在检查点自动保存 - 如果您失败了它会将您到最后一个检查点重新开始。这是一场考验您反应力的大挑战！

每一个关卡都会带给你新的挑战：更加陡峭的山坡和弯道，更加危险的地区，更多障碍 - 你根本没有时间闲得无聊。同时，每个关卡都是高清图形画质，您可以通过摄像机视角放大/缩小操作来探索世界，看到场景的更多细节。

需要有细心和耐心，您最终会挑战成功赢得比赛！

游戏玩法:

  • 操作简单容易上手
  • 非常棒的画面表现
  • 容易上瘾的游戏玩法
  • 许多有趣的关卡
  • 滑动左屏幕来滚动球
  • 滑动右侧屏幕来调整摄像机视图
