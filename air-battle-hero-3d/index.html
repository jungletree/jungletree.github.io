Air Battle Hero 3D

Become the supreme lord of the skies in the latest sky gamblers title: Air Battle Hero!

Go back in time with this great title and be part of unwritten history and covert operations. Enjoy the charm and power of the jet fighter domination race and master over a dozen airplane models.

A small group of hostile planes are scanning our land, reveal your secret military base. Their mission is to locate your base, destroy your whole country. Your job is to stand against these hostile planes, and defend your base in this 3D airplane flying game! Although all jet planes are ready to war, the truth is that the you may the only one left Airplane which is able to save us land. All pilots have been alerted, instructed and allowed to open fire against all enemy planes on sight, but only you are able to complete all missions and shoot down all enemy planes, without jeopardizing your land.

Are you ready for the ultimate 3D Flying fun? Can you save your land by playing the Air Battle Hero 3D game? Give it a try and find out at once!

Forget everything else! This is the ultimate air combat game! With an extensive simple single player campaign mode, the non-stop action will keep you at the edge of your seat!

Game Features:

  • Immersive gameplay, you are the pilot in the plane
  • Highly polished 3D Fighter Jet awaiting for flight,
  • Multiple Flying missions with lots of aerial checkpoints and other side-game missions,
  • full retro sounds
  • Dense 3D Canyon with hundreds of rocks and dangerous cliffs,
  • Unlimited Ammo for the devastating Machine Gun of your Jet Fighter plane,
  • Rechargeable set of 2 Rockets,
  • Drone attacks
  • Two different 3D camera views for showing the body or the cockpit of your Jet Fighter,
  • Easy to get start, hard to mater it
  • Smooth flights with stable plane control, multi-touch controls support.
  • Stunt Graphics, realistic environments
  • amazing 3d environments

空战英豪3D

驾驶你的战机，以惊魂速度翱翔于天际之间，穿行于层云之上。浓云密雾，完美呈现。或摧毁敌机，或悠游在云霄之上.。

与这款史诗般的游戏一起回到过去，感受未被书写的历史，加入秘密飞行行动。享受喷气式战斗机统领群雄的魅力和权力，灵活掌控驾驭战斗机的技巧: 躲闪、空战和瞄准。

一小撮敌机正在扫描我们的驻防地，它们企图揭露你的秘密军事基地。他们的任务是找到你的基地，摧毁你的国家。 在这个3D飞机飞行游戏中，而你的工作就是对付这些敌对的飞机，并捍卫你的基地！虽然所有的战斗机已经准备好进行战争，但事实上，你也许是唯一幸存的能够拯救我们陆地的战斗机飞行员。 所有的飞行员都被训话和指示，允许对所有敌机直接开火。但只有你能完成所有的任务：击落所有敌机，从而保卫你的国土。

你准备好了尝试终极3D飞行的乐趣吗？你可以玩“空战英豪3D”游戏来捍卫你的家园吗？来试试看吧！

危险无处不在。天空中到处都是虎视眈眈的敌军喷气战机， 你必将成为天空的霸主！保卫基地， 捍卫家园就看你的了，骚年！

游戏特色：

  • 飞行模拟器游戏
  • 高等抛光3D战斗机等待飞行，
  • 多个飞行任务，有许多游戏存盘点和其他支线游戏任务
  • 密集的3D峡谷：拥有数百岩石和危险悬崖
  • 为您的喷气式战斗机的毁灭性的机枪装备无限的弹药
  • 装备2组火箭弹
  • 两组不同的3D相机视图
  • 身临其境的游戏
  • 逼真的机舱驾驶操作体验
  • 平稳的飞机控制飞行
  • 操作简单便于上手，但熟练掌握需要一定的时间
  • 令人惊叹的3d环境
