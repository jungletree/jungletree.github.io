Street Light Madness

Street Light Madness is a traffic simulation game that about traffic lights, cars, or traffic control. This is a fun game in which you have to help the people cross the road in heavy traffic. Sound easy right? But can you escape the heavy traffic? There are a bunch of cars which move with different speeds. You have to carefully choose the time to move the people.

In the game, you are the traffic light adjuster at the street crossing road. Sometimes, car traffic may creates emergency situations on the road.
As a light adjuster all you need to do is switch on some lights and switch off others.
But it not so simple - cars and pedestrians are not very patient if you make them wait too long. They will run as they wish, and the situation will become very worse.

Cartoon graphics with low poly, but the real situation. Impatient drivers and pedestrians - who will crossing first? - You decide!
A good light adjuster - how long you will keep on? Go and give a try?

Game Features:

  • Infinite challenging level
  • Cartoon graphics & awesome graphics
  • Real street road situation, realistic experience of road crossing.
  • Different types of people with different walking speeds, also include different vehicles
  • Focus on the traffic signal.
  • tap on a light to open close it.

街道交通灯

这是一款关于交通灯，汽车或交通管制的交通模拟游戏。你需要成为一个有效的交通指挥员。这项工作需要真正锐利的反应和快速的反应时间。处理繁忙的交通，确保车辆畅通。这还是不是全部的内容。有时事情变得非常有趣，当你听到紧急车辆的警报时，救护车或消防车可能出现在任何地方，你的工作是让他们自由通行。紧急车辆不会停在红灯，如果你不能有效地处理交通情况，可能会造成很多麻烦。

在游戏中，你是街头十字路口的交通灯调节员。 有时候，汽车交通可能会在路上形成紧急交通事故。作为交通灯调节员，您的全部工作就是打开一些灯并或者关闭其他灯。
但这并不那么简单 - 如果你让汽车和行人等待太久，他们都会变得很没有耐心。他们会按自己的意愿肆意行走，因而交通情况会变得更糟。
游戏采用低模图形表现，但是是对交通的真实模拟。面对那些不耐烦的司机和行人 - 你会让谁会先穿过呢？ - 你决定！
作为一个好的交通灯调节员 - 你能坚持多久了？去试试看吧？

游戏特色:

  • 具有挑战性的交通突发情况
  • 真实交通路口模拟
  • 卡通渲染效果
  • 点击红绿灯来切换它
  • 现实交通流量和在急切的情况
  • 简单的操作，容易上瘾的游戏
