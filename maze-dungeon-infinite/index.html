Maze Dungeon - Infinite

If you love mazes, dungeons and adventures - this game is for you!

Your hero is a treasure hunter, who came down to the dungeon of mazes. And, as you know, mazes and dungeons always have a lot of treasures, waiting for brave heroes! But be careful, it is a rare occasion when treasures are left unguarded!

In the game you will find:

  1. The infinite number of mazes of various sizes (dungeons are randomly generated).
  2. Stone, wood and sand mazes.
  3. Wonderful treasures, the value of which depends on the complexity of mazes. Bonuses.
  4. Leaderboard - compete with other players!
  5. Flexible controls - select buttons or inclination, buttons on the right or on the left!
  6. Play the campaign or create you own maze - select the size and other parameters.

Assistants in your maze adventures:

  1. Faithful six-shooter revolver. Just in case…
  2. Labels. Will help you to mark dead-ends and other places as you wish.
  3. Flare missile. Will help you to find path and reference points.

无限地宫之解谜大师

如果你爱迷宫、地牢和冒险，那么这个游戏就是你的菜了！

你的英雄是一个宝藏猎人，他来到了迷宫的地牢。正如你所知道的，迷宫地牢总是有藏有很多的宝藏，它们等待勇敢的英雄来发掘！但要注意，这里的宝藏并非总是无人看管！

在游戏中你会发现：
1.无穷多个各种大小的迷宫（地下城是随机产生）。
2.有石材、木材和沙子的迷宫。
3.迷宫中有丰厚的宝藏和奖励，它的多少取决于迷宫的复杂性。
4.金币排行榜 - 可以与其他玩家较量一番！
5.灵活的控制 - 可以使用按钮或重力感应控制角色，同时也可以把按钮放置在右侧或左侧！
6.游戏提供闯关模式或者根据迷宫大小、以及其他参数自定义迷宫。

在你的迷宫冒险,有以下助手可以选择：
1.忠实的六发左轮手枪。以防你遇到……
2.标记。会帮助你随意标记迷宫死角、其他地方。
3.闪光弹。帮助您寻找路径和参考点。
