DEAD SLOPE 3D

DEAD SLOPE 3D is an amazing 3D game. The gameplay is very simple, just tilt the phone to move the ball to left or right to avoid obstacles.

Are you looking for a simple running game to for time killing? You found it! DEAD SLOPE 3D have a amazing and addictive gameplay! The Ball run in the city at the midnight with amazing slope paths, the controller is very simple, just tilt your phone to control the ball to run as far as you can!
Is it seems simple? Oh no! If you want a high score you must use all your best skills, by your hands, by your eyes, your sharp to control the ball avoid obstructions on the way, when you get a new high score you will be excited! Try to beat all your friend and people on the world!

Have a good time with DEAD SLOPE 3D Game!

GAME FEATURE

  • Endless running game!
  • Simple and addictive
  • Graphic in 3D
  • Global Leaderboards
  • Amazing slope paths
  • Game suitable for everyone
  • Run as far as you can

死亡下坡3D

死亡下坡3D是一款有趣的3D游戏。游戏玩法非常简单，只需倾斜手机向左或向右移动球以避开障碍物。
您是否在寻找一款简单的跑酷游戏来打发时间呢？那么现在你找到了！死亡下坡3D拥有令人惊叹且容易令人上瘾的游戏玩法！ 在午夜时分城市的上空，你操控着球在惊人的斜坡路径上极速翻滚。 控制球是非常简单，只需倾斜你的手机即可控制球运动方向，你所要做的就是尽可能地跑更远的距离！

这看起来很简单吗？ 事实并非如此！如果你想获得高分，你必须使尽浑身解数，手眼配合默契，不断磨合你对球的控制，顺利躲过路上的各种障碍物和陷阱。当你获得新的高分时你会很兴奋！游戏包含有一个全球排行榜，试着击败你所有的朋友和世界上的其他玩家！

愿你在’死亡下坡3D’游戏中玩得开心!
