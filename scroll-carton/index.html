Scroll Carton Screenshots:

Scroll Carton

Puzzle of Rolling Box in the sky
This is a simple-yet-challenging and magical 3d puzzle game. Our main character here is a carton or a cardboard box. This cardboard box stranded on a series of puzzle-shaped platforms which floating high above the earth. What you need to do is find the way to the reach the end point: a shiny blue tile. It is seems not so easy! You need to be carefull about water drops pushing, use different scrolling ways for different tiles. And do not forget to shoot out glowing purple magic stuff at crystal switches whilst to swith on some secret tiles. Make sure do not drop off from the platform, otherwise you were dead. Furthermore you could try to use steps as less as possible, manage to get full star rating. So let us play it.

Game features:

  • Colorful HD graphics
  • Simple and intuitive controls
  • Amazing level design
  • Show off your achievement to your friends

How to Play:

  • Drag or swipe to move the carton
  • Tap any where to shoot out glowing purple magic stuff

滚动的纸箱

解谜天空中3D奇幻拼图迷城上纸箱的归宿
这是一个简单而又具有挑战性和奇幻的三维益智游戏。游戏的主角是一只纸箱。这个纸板箱搁在悬浮在地球之上的由一系列拼块组成的平台上。你需要做的是找到一条路径到达终点：一个闪亮的蓝色拼块。 这似乎不是那么容易做到的！你需要小心喷出的水滴对你的推动，以及在不同的拼块使用不同的翻滚方式。不要忘了朝水晶开关上发射出紫色的魔法物品，这样能开启一些秘密的拼块路径。同时，确保不要从平台上掉下来，否则你就挂了。此外，您可以尝试尽可能以最少的步骤到达终点，争取满星级评分。现在让我们来玩玩吧。

游戏特色：

  • 丰富多彩的高清显卡
  • 简单直观的控制
  • 惊人的关卡设计
  • 向你的朋友炫耀你的成就

游戏玩法：

  • 拖动或滑动移动纸箱
  • 点击任何地方发射出紫色魔法
