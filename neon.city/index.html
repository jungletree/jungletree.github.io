Neon City

In this game you will experience yourself as a racing pilot of a space fighter. Controlling them using the accelerometer, you have to hold out as long as possible on the road, that filled with a variety of obstacles! So get it and test how long you could fly.
Features of the game:

  • Quality HD graphics in the style of the famous film Tron
  • Control by accelerometer
  • Various blocks set up in level
  • Endless game play mode
  • Uploading high scores online

霓虹都市

“霓虹都市”是一款灯光画面优美的赛车竞速类手机游戏。
在这个游戏中，你将化身为一个驾驶太空战斗机飞驰的飞行员。这款游戏运用了著名的影片Tron的风格品质的高清显示，加速度感应控制飞机的方向。你需要巧妙躲过道路上的一切障碍，并尽可能飞驰更长的距离。你可以飞多远呢？来试试吧！
