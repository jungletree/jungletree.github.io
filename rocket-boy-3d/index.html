Rocket Boy 3D

Naughty and brave Rockets boy have taken a challenge: pass through a mysterious underground maze alone. To complete the challenge and prove himself, he has to ride rockets pass through various mazes with difficulties. Rocket high speeding in the maze of the channel, avoid to crash into the maze walls, hold or release the arrow keys to choose the right path while in the fork of junction, that is what you need to do in the game. This may requires some reaction and wisdom, but I guess you should be able to deal with it easily. So, come and challenge with the rocket boy together!

How to Play:

  • Hold screen or release finger to switch the fly direction

火箭男孩
