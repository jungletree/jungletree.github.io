Hill Climb Bus Racing 3D

Hill Climb Bus Racing 3D brings you the best bus racing experience. Racing on the streets has ended. Its time to race on Hill Climb Tracks. You’ll need to master the art of control, balance, speed and drift if you want to reach the finish line on these hill climb tracks.

Hill Climb Bus Racing 3D offers a real-time multiplayer, race against your friend or against a random opponent. Its time to get on the driving chair and prove your driving skills. Driving a Bus is difficult but Racing a Bus is much more challenging.

Race through hill climb, dangerous obstacles, curvy, hilly, steep mountain road and reach your destination, sometimes you may meet a giant downhill slope in this game. The harsh truth is that if you take this seriously, you will be lost.

A very smooth gameplay brings you the real experience of driving and racing a bus. you will feel nonstop.

Remember that there are passenges on the bus ,please make them feel very safe, just like lay on the hull in a peace sea.And when you are driving the bus and climbing the hill, you need to pay more attention to the accelerometer, if the accelerometer is zero, that will be dangerous.

Realistic 3D environment brings more life to the game, 3 different themes to play, and lots of buses including omnibus to upgrade.

Start the extreme racing experience and perform insane driving control on some of the most difficult hill climb tracks.

  • Free to Play!

  • Wonderful Interiors
  • Multiple controls, Steering Wheel, Buttons or Tilting
  • Intelligent Traffic System
  • Challenge your friends with realtime multiplayer

  • Single Player and multiplayer Mode

爬山巴士赛车3D

爬山巴士赛车3D将会带给你最好的巴士赛车体验。街上的巴士模拟已经不好玩了，现在是在狭窄的山道上爬山比赛。你需要掌握控制、平衡、速度和漂移的艺术，如果你想达到这些山坡攀山峰的终点。

爬山巴士赛车3D提供了一个实时的多人游戏，你可以与您的朋友或随机对手进行比赛。现在坐上驾驶椅，证明你的驾驶技术了。我们知道驾驶公共汽车是困难的，但驾驶公共汽车进行竞速爬坡比赛是更具挑战性。

比赛通过爬山，危险的障碍，弯曲，丘陵，陡峭的山路，并达到您的目的地。有时候你会在这个游戏中遇到一个巨大的下坡，稍有不慎，你就会挑战失败，跌落悬崖。

请记住，车上有旅客，你需要让他们感到很安全，就像躺在平静的海面上。当你在驾驶公共汽车和爬山的时候，你需要更加注意加速表，如果加速度计是零，那将是危险的。

现实的3D环境为游戏带来了更多的活力，3个不同的主题可以玩，还有包括公共汽车在内的很多公交车可以升级。

现在让我们开始极限赛车体验，并且驾驶公共汽车进行竞速爬坡比赛吧。
