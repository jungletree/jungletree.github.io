Zombie Reaper 3D: Horror Strike

You may the last survivor of your team,and the last human in this city,give up? No! It’s the time to be the grim reaper in this Zombie Hell,blow the heads of them,bring them “home”!

Zombies are weak at the neck and head part, so try to shoot them at head part to see the head shot effect.
Remember that don’t let them bite you.

Tips:

  • Shoot the head of them, then one bullet can make target dead
  • Shoot fast, then you can get higher score
  • Bullet can pass through walls, then you can kill zombies without seeing them
  • Grenades only kill zombies, so you can throw it freely into the “dead”

僵尸收割者3D: 死亡恐怖来袭

僵尸狂潮再度来袭，小镇仿佛一夜之间变为死城。而你和其他特战成员在潜入小镇执行营救任务失败，不幸被包围，战友全部牺牲，作为唯一的幸存者，放弃？不。。。你要一战成神！

游戏玩法:

  • 僵尸的头部非常脆弱，一颗子弹就可以搞定
  • 尽可能快速射击，那么您可以得到高分
  • 子弹都可以穿墙，所以你可以在看见它们之前就干掉它们
  • 手雷伤害只会针对僵尸，所以你随意地使用手雷
