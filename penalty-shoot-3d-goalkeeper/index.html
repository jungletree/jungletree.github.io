Penalty shoot 3D : Goalkeeper

Soccer champions aren’t born yet, but it will. It is time for intense penalty shooting.

You，as the goalkeeper, stand still in front of the goal, watch closely where the ball it and predict how the ball fly into the goal. Suddenly, you jump up and push the ball out of the goal with your gloves precisely. The ball is not in goal！The whole football field of audiences are cheering. That moment you just like an experienced hunter shoot out the fatal blow to its prey. All kind of ball kick tricks such as ground ball,header,bicycle kick,chest-high ball,volley,slide tackle and so on are useless to you. Because you are the best goalkeeper with gold gloves honor! Now it’s your turn to start write your own goalkeeper legend.

How to Play:

  • click and drag gloves to the incoming ball
